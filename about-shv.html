About Us

SHV is situated on Karuvalur - Annur – Avinashi Main Road, (SH69). The school under the auspices HT promoted by well exposed and experienced teachers, business people and philanthropists. The trustees have a unified vision to promote a unique institution to develop responsible citizens for the country. The Vidhyalaya aims to train every student to rise to the greatest achievement in their chosen area of interest. SHV embarks to provide right direction in the early stages an environment that stimulates creativity, innovation and confidence to strengthen their talents. “Researchers in child psychology have recorded that “the formative period of the child is very important and the quality learning experience in the early stages proves for positive development”.

Under the able leadership of SRI HAYAKGREVAR TRUST, the school has flourished as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development. The trust's visionary approach to education has led to the establishment of a nurturing and innovative learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential, think critically, and develop a strong sense of social responsibility. With a focus on continuous improvement, the trust strives to stay at the forefront of educational best practices, ensuring that the school remains a hub of intellectual growth and character development.

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya

SRI HAYAKGREVAR VIDHYALAYA MATRIC. HR. SEC. SCHOOL was establised in 2013 and operated by SRI HAYAKGREVAR TRUST. This School is located in Pups Karuvalur, Uppilipalayam, Avinashi, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The school is Co-educational, Matric Curriculum and English is the medium of instructions in this school.
