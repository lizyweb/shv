About Us

Co-educational, Matric Curriculum and English

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya Matric Higher Secondary School

SRI HAYAKGREVAR VIDHYALAYA MATRIC. HR. SEC. SCHOOL, established in 2013 and managed by SRI HAYAKGREVAR TRUST, is situated in a rural area in the AVINASHI block of COIMBATORE district, Tamil Nadu. The school offers education from Grades 1 to 12 and includes a pre-primary section. The medium of instruction is English, and the academic session commences in April. Accessible via an all-weather road, the school features a private building with 20 well-maintained classrooms and 2 additional rooms for non-teaching activities. It also provides a separate room for the Headmaster/Teacher and is equipped with a pucca boundary wall, electricity connection, and a playground.

The school's library houses 750 books, and it has 12 functional computers for teaching and learning purposes. While there's no computer-aided learning lab, the school does not offer a mid-day meal program. It provides 10 functional boys' toilets and 10 girls' toilets with a tap water source. In terms of academics, the school is located in Avinashi, Tiruppur, and boasts 47 dedicated faculty members with a student-teacher ratio of 11:1. Known for its exceptional academic results and excellence in extracurricular activities, it serves approximately 520 students from the region.

The school is proudly managed by the esteemed SRI HAYAKGREVAR TRUST, an organization dedicated to fostering excellence in education and nurturing the future leaders of our society. With a rich history dating back several decades, the trust has consistently demonstrated a strong commitment to providing quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in today's ever-changing world.

Quick Information

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya offers a wide range of co-curricular activities to enrich students' educational experiences. These activities include sports, arts, and community service opportunities. Participation in co-curricular activities is encouraged to promote holistic development and personal growth, fostering well-rounded individuals within our school community.

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya boasts state-of-the-art labs, including a well-equipped Computer Lab for digital learning, along with specialized Physics, Chemistry, and Biology Labs designed to facilitate hands-on experimentation and scientific exploration, enhancing the practical learning experience for our students.

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya enforces strict rules to ensure a safe and respectful learning environment. Students must adhere to attendance policies, maintain proper dress code, exhibit good behavior, uphold academic integrity, and respect others while abiding by technology, health, and property guidelines. Violations may result in consequences such as warnings, detention, suspension, or expulsion.

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya proudly boasts a well-stocked library containing a collection of 750 books. This valuable resource is accessible to students and faculty, fostering a culture of reading, research, and academic exploration within our school community.

Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya provides transportation services to ensure the safe and convenient commute of our students. Our fleet of dedicated buses is equipped with safety measures and professional drivers to facilitate daily transportation to and from the school, ensuring that students have reliable access to their educational environment.

Weekly extracurricular classes in dance, skating, Silambam, Karate, and Araneri are offered to all students at Sri Hayakgrevar Vidhyalaya, promoting their overall personality development alongside academics.
